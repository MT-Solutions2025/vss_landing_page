Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01-12-2025

At MT Solution, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information provided by users of our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Vehicle information such as entry and exit logs.
  • Service and financial data related to services provided.
  • Employee attendance and work schedules (if applicable).
  • Resource consumption data such as water and electricity usage.
  • Images or video captured by cameras for operational and administrative purposes only.
  • Contact information provided by users, including name, email, and phone number.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and improve vehicle service and car wash operations.
  • To generate financial and operational reports.
  • To monitor performance and maintain employee accountability.
  • To optimize resource usage, including electricity and water.
  • To support informed business decisions and enhance user experience.

3. Data Protection

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our systems comply with applicable data protection laws and standards to ensure confidentiality.

4. Sharing Your Information

  • With explicit consent from the user.
  • When required by law or government authorities in Qatar.

5. Use of Cameras

Cameras are used solely for operational and administrative purposes. They are not intended to violate individual privacy, and it is the responsibility of the business owner to inform staff and visitors about their presence according to local regulations.

6. User Rights

  • Access their personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request suspension of data usage under agreed terms.

7. Updates to This Policy

MT Solution reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of any significant changes through official channels.
