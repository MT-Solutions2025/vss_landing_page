VSS – Smart Vehicle Service & Car Wash Management

Manage your vehicle service center effortlessly with VSS, the all-in-one digital solution for car wash and vehicle service operations. Designed for efficiency, accuracy, and growth, VSS empowers businesses to streamline operations and deliver superior service.

Key Features

  • Real-time Vehicle Tracking: Monitor cars entering and exiting your service center seamlessly.
  • AI-Powered ANPR Camera Integration: Automate license plate recognition and service logging for faster operations.
  • Employee & Attendance Management: Track staff performance, schedules, and attendance easily.
  • Inventory & Resource Control: Manage stock, water, and electricity consumption efficiently to reduce waste.
  • Instant Notifications: Receive alerts for service updates, milestones, and operational events in real-time.
  • Reports & Analytics: Gain actionable insights to make informed business decisions.

Why Choose VSS?

VSS streamlines your workflow, reduces operational waste, and improves service efficiency—all within a secure, user-friendly platform. It is ideal for car washes, vehicle service centers, and multi-location operations seeking intelligent, data-driven solutions.
